Who We Are

We are a local team of travel lovers, historians, photographers, and cultural ambassadors, united by one mission: to share Cambodia’s breathtaking beauty and spirit with the world.

Our Story

Cambodia Travel was founded in 2015 with a passion for showcasing Cambodia’s rich heritage and natural beauty. From the awe-inspiring temples of Angkor Wat to the serene beaches of Sihanoukville, we craft unforgettable journeys that connect travelers with the soul of Cambodia. Our team is dedicated to sharing the Kingdom of Wonder with the world.

Our Vision

We believe in sustainable tourism that benefits local communities, respects cultural heritage, and protects our natural treasures.

  • Empowering local guides & businesses
  • Eco-friendly tours & experiences
  • Celebrating Khmer traditions & food
